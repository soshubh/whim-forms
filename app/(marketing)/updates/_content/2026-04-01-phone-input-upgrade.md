Phone fields now support split country code and local number behavior, custom dropdown styling, digit-only input, and controlled length handling.

## What changed

- Country code and local number handling are now more structured.
- Input length and digit constraints are handled more clearly.
- The phone control now behaves more like a purpose-built field instead of a loose text input.

## Why it matters

Phone capture is one of the most fragile parts of a lead form. This update makes the field more usable in the builder and more reliable in the exported result.
